SERVICES

    Booking Orders

    • Raw Master is an online platforms that will allow consumers
      to book their orders online this to ease and a way of maximizes
      their time.

    Delivery Service

    • Raw Master offers a same day delivery service to our consumers
      Delivery time will 45 minutes to 1 hour from the time of placing your
      order in our system.

    Cancel Orders

    • Raw Master allows cancellation of orders, orders will be posible
      to cancel once the order is not yet tag as in transit status
      or else orders can no longer be canceled.

    Redelivery Service

    • Raw Master offers a Redelivery service to customers in case of
      disatisfaction of the product quality upon receiving the order
      customer will just document what is wrong with the product.
      customer will keep the damage product and request for a Redelivery.

    Refund Service

    • Raw Master offers a Refund service to customers in case of
      disatisfaction of the product quality upon receiving the order
      most especially if product received has a foul odor or insects
      or paste is in the product or any forms of dirt in the product
      customer will just document what is wrong with the product.
      customer will keep the damage product and request for product
      Redelivery.

    Dicounts Service

    • Raw Master offers offers a dicount to loyal customer who have
      made multiple orders in our system. once customer have place
      multiple orders customer will be given a 10% discounts effective
      for the next orders.
